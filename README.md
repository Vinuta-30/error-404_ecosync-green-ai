# ECOSYNC 

## Overview
EcoSync is a self-aware Green AI system that helps cities manage electricity intelligently. It predicts energy demand, prevents blackouts, reduces carbon emissions, and shifts non-essential loads to cleaner energy hours.

## Problem Statement
As AI systems, EV charging, and smart city infrastructure grow, electricity demand rises rapidly. This can overload power grids, cause blackouts, and increase dependence on fossil fuels.

## Our Solution
EcoSync continuously monitors city energy demand and makes smart real-time decisions:
- Protects hospitals and critical services
- Reduces non-essential load during peak demand
- Delays flexible tasks like EV charging
- Maximizes renewable energy usage
- Lowers total carbon emissions

## Key Features
- Full-day energy demand prediction
-  Carbon emission reduction analysis
-  Smart load balancing recommendations
-  Priority support for hospitals
-  Sustainable grid optimization
-  Transparent AI decision-making
